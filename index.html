About Us

Ours is a story tied to India, the oldest culture in word the ancient mythological country that has seen us be born and grow. Presently one of the fastest growing economy and having the highest infrastructure growth need. Our name "Bhoj" originates from historical roots sown by the greatest emperor of Bhopal, now the capital of Madhya Pradesh.

We base our roots on a solid past of education and experience, our values: honesty, integrity and respect for people, that have the merit of being always current: passion and innovation, generating our commitment "Think Global Act Local". This is why we have never stopped growing and learning.....

To the world ours is the story of a company but for us it is also the story of brotherhood between friends: a excursion with difficult climbs, exciting flights and fights but above all with many exciting successes gained by exceptional teammates.

We want to maximize growth and efficiency and not just profits, focusing on a mix that includes products, application and sound technical people. Our aim is to provide solutions consistent with local needs always keeping the costs under control with use of latest global technology.

Bhoj Rasayana is indulged in providing technical services and on site design of Concrete Admixtures, Curing Compounds, Application Service, Providing of an impeccable array of Coatings & Wrappings, Roofing & Cladding, Structural Rehabilitation & Retro Fittings, Water Proofing Systems, Engineering, Construction & Contracts, Epoxy & PU Coatings and Pressure Grouting.

The provided services are extensively admired for its speed and flexibility, well-timed execution and promptness. These services are carried out using hi-tech equipment, modern tools and contemporary machinery, which account for superlative finishing and efficiency offered by them. Additionally, our hard-working team of quality controllers and engineers is precise about the quality parameters of the services and confirms their application.
